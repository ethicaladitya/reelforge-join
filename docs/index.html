What's included

Everything a creator needs to ship

Built for people who want to ship content fast without sacrificing quality.

AI Word-Level Captions

Faster-Whisper transcribes every word. Captions animate in sync with keyword highlighting to keep viewers watching.

EBU R128 Audio Normalization

Every clip is loudness-normalized to broadcast standard so volume stays consistent across your entire reel.

Automatic Silence Trimming

Dead air at the start and end of clips is detected and removed automatically so your pacing stays tight.

Smooth Transitions

Crossfade video and audio transitions between clips for a seamless, professional look without any manual work.

Cinematic Ken Burns Zoom

A subtle pan-and-zoom effect adds production value and keeps the frame dynamic, even for static shots.

Vertical-First Output

All output is 1080x1920 at 30fps — the native format for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

Background Music Mix

Upload your own music track and ReelForge mixes it under your voice at the right level automatically.

Personal Reel Library

Every rendered reel is saved to your library. Preview, download, or re-publish any reel at any time.
